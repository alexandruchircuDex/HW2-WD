Our Story

Dexter's Brew Café opened in 2024 as a small family-owned coffee shop. The goal was to create a welcoming place where customers can relax, study, or meet friends while enjoying quality drinks and desserts.

What We Offer

  • Specialty coffee and tea
  • Fresh pastries,desserts and food
  • Free Wi-Fi access
  • Comfortable indoor seating
  • Student discounts
